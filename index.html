\[ C = \epsilon_0 \epsilon_r \frac{S}{2 \lambda_D} \]

\[ \lambda_D = \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon_0 \epsilon_r k_B T}{2 c_\infty}} \]

Poisson-Nernst-Planck (Stokes)

\[ \frac{\partial c_i}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot J_i \]

\[ J_i = uc_i - D_i \nabla c_i - \frac{D_iz_ie}{k_\mathrm{B} T}c_i\nabla \psi \]

\[ \nabla^2 \psi = - \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0 \epsilon_r} = - \frac{1}{\epsilon_0 \epsilon_r} \sum_i c_i z_i e \]

Etat stationnaire: Poisson-Boltzmann

\[ \nabla^2\psi=-\frac{1}{\epsilon_0\epsilon_r} \sum_i c_{i,\infty}z_ie \exp \left( \frac{-z_ie\psi}{k_{\rm B}T} \right) \]

Etat stationnaire: Debye-Hückel

\[ \nabla^2\psi=-\frac{1}{\epsilon_0\epsilon_r} \sum_i c_{i,\infty}z_ie \exp \left( \frac{-z_ie\psi}{k_{\rm B}T} \right) \]

\[ \psi << \frac{k_B T}{e} \approx 25mV \]

\[ \nabla^2 \psi = \frac{1}{\lambda_D^2} \psi \]

Méthodes utilisées
