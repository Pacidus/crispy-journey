The idea

Macroscopic

Hydrodynamic description of nonideal fluid mixtures
\[ \rho \left(\frac{\partial \vec{u}}{\partial t} + \vec{u} \cdot \vec{\nabla} \vec{u} \right) = \nu \vec{\nabla}^2 \vec{u} - \sum_k \rho_k \vec{\nabla} \beta \mu_k + \vec{F}^\text{ext} \] \[ \vec{\nabla} \cdot \vec{u} = 0, \]

Macroscopic

Poisson-Nernst-Planck
\[ \frac{\partial \rho_s \vec{u}}{\partial t} = \nu \vec{\nabla}^2 \vec{u} - \vec{\nabla} P - k_B T (\rho_+ + \rho_-) \vec{\nabla}C - k_B T (\rho_+ - \rho_-) \vec{\nabla}V, \] \[ \frac{\partial \rho_\pm}{\partial t} + \vec{\nabla} \cdot \left[ \rho_\pm \vec{u} - D_\pm \rho_\pm \vec{\nabla}(\ln \rho_\pm + C) \mp D_\pm \rho_\pm \vec{\nabla}(\varphi +V) \right] = 0, \] \[ \vec{\nabla}^2 \varphi = - \kappa^2 \sinh \varphi. \]

Mesoscopic

Fluid dynamics

Navier-Stokes $\rightarrow$ Boltzmann equation

\[ \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} + \vec{v} \cdot \vec{\nabla}_{\vec{r}} f + \frac{\vec{F}_\text{ext}}{\rho} \cdot \vec{\nabla}_{\vec{v}} f = C(f) \]

with $C(f) := -\tau^{-1}(f-f^{eq})$ the BGK operator
